About Old durbar hotel

Our story and commitment to authentic flavors

Where Tradition Meets Modern Taste

Established in 2010, Old durbar hotel has been serving the Itahari community with authentic Nepali cuisine made with love and tradition.

We pride ourselves on using traditional recipes passed down through generations, combined with fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

At Old durbar hotel, we believe that good food brings people together. Whether you're enjoying a quick breakfast, a leisurely lunch, or a family dinner, we strive to make every visit memorable.

Award Winning

Recognized as "Best hotel in Itahari" for three consecutive years (2020-2023).

Community Focused

We actively support local farmers and participate in community events.

Family Friendly

Our warm and welcoming environment is perfect for families of all sizes.
